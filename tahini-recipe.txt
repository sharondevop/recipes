Ingredients:
  - 2 cups tahini sesame seed paste(made from brown color seeds)
  - 1 cloves raw garlic
  - 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (or more to taste)
  - 1/4 tsp salt (or more to taste)
  - 1 1/2 cups of water (filter water)


Directions:
    1. Stir vigorously tahini with 1/2 cups of water until the desired proximity.

    2. Add lemon juice, garlic and salt, stir with 1 cups of water.
